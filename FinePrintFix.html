What we flag

We catch what most people miss

Non-competes & restrictions
Clauses that limit what jobs you can take or businesses you can start after leaving.
Forced arbitration
Language that takes away your right to sue and locks you into private dispute resolution.
Auto-renewals & cancellation traps
Subscriptions or contracts that roll over automatically and are hard to exit.
Data & privacy clauses
What they can collect, share, or sell about you — buried in the fine print.
One-sided change rights
When a company can change the terms whenever they want without telling you.
