FAQ

Common questions

Why no subscription?

Most people sign a handful of contracts a year — a job offer, a lease, a freelance agreement. A subscription model means you'd pay every month for something you might use twice. We think that's the wrong model for this product. Pay when you have a document in front of you, keep your money the rest of the time.

What's the real difference between Standard and Professional?

Standard gives you a solid overview — the most important flags, key sections, questions to ask, and negotiation scripts. It's designed for most personal documents where you want to understand what you're signing.

Professional removes every cap and goes exhaustive — it reads every clause, gives you a risk score out of 100 with a breakdown, flags everything missing from the document that should be there, and gives you actual replacement contract language ready to paste into a redline. It's designed for documents where you have real leverage and the stakes are high enough to negotiate.

Is this legal advice?

No. FinePrintFix is an AI-powered analysis tool for informational purposes only. It helps you understand what a document says and flags potential issues — it is not a substitute for advice from a qualified attorney. For high-stakes documents, we recommend using FinePrintFix as preparation before consulting an attorney, not instead of one.

Do you store my documents?

No. Your original documents — PDFs, text, whatever you upload — are sent to the AI for analysis and immediately discarded. We never store the source document. What gets saved to your dashboard (if you have an account) is only the analysis output: the flags, summary, questions, and financial breakdown. Per Anthropic's API policy, data sent via their API is not used to train AI models.

Can I get a refund?

All sales are final for completed analyses since the service is delivered immediately and digitally. If you were charged due to a technical error, or charged more than once for the same analysis, we will refund you in full. Contact hello@fineprintfix.com within 7 days with your payment details.

What document types can I analyze?

Any legal document in PDF format or pasted text. This includes employment contracts, leases, contractor agreements, freelance contracts, NDAs, severance agreements, terms of service, subscription agreements, privacy policies, service contracts, and more. If it's a legal document you're being asked to sign or agree to, FinePrintFix can analyze it.

How long does an analysis take?

Standard analyses typically complete in under 90 seconds. Professional analyses are more exhaustive and may take 2–3 minutes depending on document length. Comparison pack analyses (up to 3 documents) take approximately 3–5 minutes total since documents are analyzed sequentially.
