The honest truth about ToS

You can't negotiate it. But you can understand it.

Most terms of service are take-it-or-leave-it. You're not going to negotiate with Netflix or your gym. But knowing what you agreed to — before or after clicking accept — still matters. It affects your data rights, your ability to cancel, and what happens when something goes wrong.

Without FinePrintFix

"Terms of Service — I agree."

You find out 14 months later that they've been auto-renewing at a higher price, sold your data to advertising partners, and that you can't sue them — because you agreed to binding arbitration on page 34.

With FinePrintFix

"This service auto-renews annually on March 12. Cancel by March 5 to avoid the next charge. They share behavioral data with 14 ad partners. You've waived your right to sue — disputes go to private arbitration. Content library is not guaranteed."

Now you decide with full information.

What we flag

The things buried on page 34

These aren't edge cases. They appear in the terms of almost every major subscription service, app, and platform you use.

Auto-renewal traps
When it renews, how much notice you need to cancel, and whether you get a refund if you miss the window. Many services make cancellation intentionally difficult.
Arbitration clauses
Most ToS waive your right to sue in court and participate in class action lawsuits. This is a significant legal rights waiver most people don't realize they've made.
Data selling & sharing
Whether your personal data, browsing behavior, or usage patterns can be sold or shared with third parties — including advertising networks and data brokers.
Unilateral price increases
Whether they can raise prices without your active consent, and how much notice they're required to give before your next billing date.
Terms can change anytime
Most services can change their terms at any time, with continued use constituting acceptance. You may be agreeing to future terms you haven't seen yet.
Account termination rights
Under what circumstances they can terminate your account, whether you get a refund, and what happens to your data and content if they do.
Content & IP rights
For platforms where you upload content, what license you're granting the company to use, distribute, or modify what you create.
Governing law
Which state or country's laws apply and where disputes must be resolved. This affects what consumer protections you actually have.
Exit awareness

Know how to leave before you need to

FinePrintFix extracts the cancellation and exit terms so you always know the answers to these questions before you're scrambling to find them.

1
When does it renew?
Exact renewal date, whether monthly or annual, and whether the price can change at renewal.
2
How do you cancel?
The specific steps required — whether it's a button in settings, a phone call, or something designed to be hard to find.
3
What's the cancellation deadline?
How many days before renewal you need to cancel to avoid being charged for the next period.
4
Will you get a refund?
Whether partial refunds exist, under what conditions, and what the actual refund process looks like.
5
What happens to your data?
Whether your data is deleted after cancellation, how long they retain it, and how to request deletion under CCPA or GDPR.
