What we flag

Subscription traps that cost businesses and consumers thousands

Auto-renewal terms
How far in advance you must cancel, what the renewal price is, and whether they're required to notify you before it happens.
Early termination fees
What it costs to leave before your contract ends — some charge 50-100% of remaining contract value.
Data sharing clauses
What they can collect, share, or sell about your usage — and whether you can opt out.
No-refund policies
Whether any payments are refundable and under what circumstances — including after accidental renewal.
Price increase rights
Whether the vendor can raise prices at renewal and how much notice they must give you.
Feature modification rights
Whether they can remove features or change the service during your contract term without your consent.
Arbitration clauses
Whether disputes must go to arbitration rather than courts — and who pays the filing fees.
What's standard and fair
We tell you which clauses are normal so you know exactly what's worth pushing back on.
Negotiation scripts

Questions to ask before you commit

More is negotiable than most people realize — especially for annual or multi-year commitments.

1
"Can we change the auto-renewal cancellation window from 60 days to 30 days, and require a written reminder 45 days before renewal?"
2
"Can we cap the early termination fee at unused months only, rather than 50% of the remaining contract value?"
3
"Can you confirm that our usage data will not be shared with third-party advertising or analytics partners?"
4
"Can we add a 30-day refund window for accidental renewals, provided we haven't used the service after renewal?"
5
"Can we add a price lock clause — no increases during the initial contract term?"
